About me

I've started Front end web development some months ago, after a long professional experience as a Building Engineer.

I learnt by myself time ago how to create a web-site with Wordpress but then I discovered that there was a huge world behind it. A world full of challenges and self-learning which could open me a new opportunity to develop myself in the web world: to become a Web Developer.

First I began learning some of HTML and CSS to change the design of my webs, It was amazing and made some plugins of Wordpress useless.

Then I learned to code. I started with Python but I soon discovered that I would need JavaScript to animate the web pages. Then I got captivated by JavaScript.

Now I'm learning as much I can about "web development" in "Freecodecamp", "Codecademy", "Udemy", and other resources... while I study in a Bootcamp from the "Commerce Chamber" and the "Telefonica Foundation"

I'm looking for an opportunity to get into the professional environment of the web development world.

I have the opportunity to do an internship in Europe thanks to an agreement that the "Commerce Chamber" could do with other enterprises.

If you are looking for a Junior Developer, self-motivated and passionate about the web, just contact me!
